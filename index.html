    The Vision

    • Product Features:
    • Stick Ordering Made Simple
    • Customizable Flex
    • Low Weight
    • Blade Lie

    Heritage Hockey was founded on the idea that hockey players are paying too much money for leading brands of hockey sticks. What is more is that the high costs are not justified by improved performance, or improved product life - the product offering is the same as it has been for years, and the costs have only increased. Our mission is to solve this problem.

    We will be offering a unique service where players are able to purchase hockey sticks on a regular basis. Due to the low cost, players will be able to either swap out sticks they do not care for , or replace them as soon as they lose their “pop”. As hockey players ourselves, we understand the need for this service and also understand the need for our sticks to perform as we have come to expect. The sticks we sell you will feel every bit as responsive as a leading brand. No stick will be sold that is not personally tested by the owners. Period. We would not use a product that would compromise our game, nor would we sell one to the hockey community.
